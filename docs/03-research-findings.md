## 4. Hype-cycle cohorts

- **Dot-com:** 476 IPOs in 1999, mean day-1 return **71%** (Ritter); 1999–2000 avg ~65% vs ~15% in 1990–98. Nasdaq -75%+ Mar 2000–Oct 2002; ~$1.755T internet-stock value erased by Nov 2000. *Gap:* precise "% below offer by 2002" not yet computed — see research plan WP-1.
- **2020–21 wave:** 613 SPAC IPOs in 2021. By Mar 2023 only **~5%** of the 2020–21 IPO/SPAC class traded above offer; de-SPAC mergers lost avg 59–67%; post-merger SPACs underperformed Nasdaq by 44%.
- **2025–26 AI cohort:** CoreWeave +107% vs IPO but -55% off peak (founders sold $2.3B); Figma +250% day-1 then -81% to below offer; Cerebras +68% day-1 at ~110x sales with 86% UAE revenue concentration flagged; Databricks ~$134B listing pending. 2025 avg day-1 return 24% — but **8% excluding Figma and Circle** (ION). Pops are concentrated, not broad — unlike 1999.
